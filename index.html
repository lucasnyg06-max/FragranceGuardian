You've spent hundreds building your collection. Every trip puts it at risk.

Checked bags get thrown. Cabin pressure loosens caps. And there's no product on the market actually designed for fragrance bottles. Until now.

The risk

Glass doesn't forgive

One rough baggage handler. One tumble in an overhead bin. A $200 bottle of Baccarat Rouge becomes a stain on your clothes and a story you wish you didn't have.

The hack

Socks aren't a strategy

We've all done it. Wrap it in a t-shirt, stuff it in a shoe, stick it in a ziplock. It works until it doesn't. And you never know which trip will be the one.

The fix

A case that actually fits

Fragrance Guardian is shaped around bottles, not the other way around. Shock-absorbing walls, a seal that prevents leaks, and a footprint that fits in any carry-on.

Designed for the way collectors actually travel

We talked to hundreds of fragrance enthusiasts before sketching a single line. Every detail comes from a real frustration.

1

Bottle-specific cradles

Adjustable inserts that hold 50ml to 100ml bottles upright and snug. No rattling, no shifting, no contact between glass surfaces.

2

Shock-absorbing shell

Multi-layer walls designed to absorb impact from drops, tosses, and the general violence of airline baggage handling.

3

Leak-proof seal

Even if a cap loosens, the sealed interior keeps everything contained. Your clothes stay clean. Your luggage stays dry.

4

Carry-on friendly

Compact enough to tuck into a weekender or a carry-on pocket. No wasted space, no awkward shapes.

5

Opens in two seconds

Magnetic closure. Pop it open, swap your bottle, close it, go. No zippers to fumble, no latches to break.
